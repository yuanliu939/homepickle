Buyer Profile

Describe your situation, preferences, and concerns in your own words. This text is included in every AI property evaluation to personalize the analysis. Auto-saves as you type.

Suggestions for your profile

Include anything that matters to you. Here are some ideas:

  • Workplace address — for commute analysis (e.g. "I work at 500 Howard Street, San Francisco, CA 94105")
  • Current address — where you live now, for context
  • Commute preferences — max commute time, preferred modes (BART, Caltrain, driving), tolerance for transfers
  • Budget & financing — price range, down payment %, loan type
  • Size requirements — min bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage, garage, yard
  • Location preferences — preferred neighborhoods, proximity to amenities, walkability
  • School requirements — school district quality, specific schools, grade levels
  • Risk concerns — fire zone, flood zone, earthquake, insurance costs
  • Lifestyle — quiet neighborhood, near parks/trails, pet-friendly, HOA tolerance
  • Property condition — move-in ready vs. renovation OK, age preferences
  • Dealbreakers — anything that's an absolute no-go
